DAILY STUDENT FOOTPRINT BENCHMARKS:
- Low impact lifestyle: 4-6 kg CO2/day
- Average Indian student: 8-12 kg CO2/day
- High consumption (AC + car + meat): 15-25 kg CO2/day

REDUCTION STRATEGIES (ranked by impact):
1. Switch 50% of trips to bus/metro/cycle: saves 2-4 kg CO2/day
2. Reduce red meat to once a week: saves 3-5 kg CO2/week
3. Turn off AC when room is empty: saves 5-10 kg CO2/day
4. Use laptop power saving mode: saves 0.5 kg CO2/day
5. Buy local/seasonal food in cafeteria: saves 0.5-1 kg CO2/day
6. Avoid single-use plastic: saves 0.2 kg CO2/item

CAMPUS INITIATIVES:
- Solar panels on academic blocks reduce grid dependency by 30-40%
- Tree plantation: one tree absorbs ~22 kg CO2/year
- Paperless assignments save ~0.5 kg CO2 per student per semester
- LED lighting upgrade saves 60% energy vs incandescent
